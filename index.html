About Me

I’m Raj Tiwari, a Senior Backend Developer with over a decade of experience building and maintaining production-grade web applications.

My primary expertise is in Laravel and PHP, where I design clean, secure, and scalable backend architectures for e-commerce, CMS, and enterprise platforms. Alongside PHP, I also work with Node.js and Python for automation, microservices, scripting, and data-driven workflows—choosing the right tool based on the problem, not preference.

In recent years, my focus has shifted toward backend automation, AI API integrations, and data extraction pipelines. I build systems that automate repetitive processes, integrate AI services, and handle large-scale data processing to improve operational efficiency.

I have hands-on experience managing AWS infrastructure, optimizing application performance, and deploying secure, scalable systems using modern DevOps practices. I’m comfortable owning projects end-to-end—from system design and development to deployment, monitoring, and optimization.

I believe in clean code, clear architecture, and practical solutions. My goal is to build backend systems that are reliable, maintainable, and ready to scale as business needs grow.
